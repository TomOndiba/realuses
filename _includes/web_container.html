The Web

I designed the internal content structure for several pages of the Ark V2 prototype. My task was to create a concept of what the 'Shop' section of the site would look like. The first active sub-navigation module was 'Featured' which was designed to show off some of the top products offered by featured stores on Ark's network. 'All Stores' features the ability to view the featured products within a carousel on mouse-over. 'Today's Deals' focuses heavily on location-based personalization.
